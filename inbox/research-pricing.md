# Deep-Research — Pricing page patterns (2026-06-18)

> deep-research harness (102 agents, 20 sources, 82 claims → 25 verified, 11 confirmed / 14 killed). For the Ledenov pricing page.

## Verdict
**Do NOT build a price calculator/estimator.** None of the leading productized solo/small design services (Designjoy, Artone, UI Flip) use one — they publish a flat price (or "starting at" floor) and route custom/scoped work to a call or human quote. For a senior solo designer, the proven pattern is **transparent single-anchor pricing + "from $X" floor + call-based scoping**, not a calculator.

## Confirmed patterns (verified against live sites, June 2026)
1. **Publish price openly** (transparency = the conversion lever). Designjoy $4,995/mo, Artone $4,000/mo, UI Flip "from $6,000/mo" — all public, no gate. [designjoy.co, artone.studio, uiflip.com]
2. **No calculator.** Fixed price + a bulleted "what's included" list; custom needs → 15-min intro call / quote. [designjoy.co, artone.studio]
3. **Risk-reduction terms on the offer.** "Pause or cancel anytime", partial money-back ("75% back in week 1"), free no-card trial. Lowers commitment anxiety. [designjoy.co, uiflip.com]
4. **Cost-comparison vs hiring.** Artone contrasts "Traditional hire: $120K+/yr, slow, locked-in contracts" vs "Artone: $X/mo, flexible, senior." Anchors and justifies price. → use a **vs full-time hire / vs agency** comparison, NOT a feature-matrix table. [artone.studio]
5. **Retainer/subscription framing** (flat fee, steady cadence, pause/cancel) dominates over per-project scoping among the leaders.

## ⚠️ Refuted (do NOT rely on — vendor-blog numerology, killed 0-3 under verification)
- "3-tier pages convert 1.4×", "Most Popular badge lifts conversion", "decoy/anchor highest tier", "reducing tiers 5→3 lifts conversion", "30-day money-back +X%", "action CTA beats generic +X%", "calculator generates X% more leads". No traceable primary A/B studies. Don't cite percentages.

## Caveats
- Evidence is **descriptive** (what leaders do), not controlled experiments for a solo designer. Comparable retainers ($4–6k/mo) are larger than this client's $1–4k/wk — the *structure* transfers, not the absolute numbers.
- Validate against the client's own funnel before treating as optimized. A solo operator may gain more from qualitative founder feedback than CRO experiments.

## Recommended pricing page (this case)
1. Hero: "Simple, senior, no surprises" + open-pricing promise.
2. Three offers with **"what's included" bullets**: Free UX audit · Project (from $500) · Ongoing ($1,000/week, the hero anchor).
3. **Cost comparison** vs full-time hire / agency / junior-DIY → positions value.
4. **Trust strip**: scoped on a call, NDA always, start within a week, stop anytime.
5. Pricing FAQ. No calculator.
